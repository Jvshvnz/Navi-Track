About the Project

Built for Pangasinan State University

Navi Track was created by frontend developers and PSU students to solve real campus pain points: lost time finding rooms, clashing schedules, and manual lab attendance sheets.

Version 2.4  ·  May 2026

Problem Statement

PSU Bayambang students and faculty face three recurring challenges: navigating unfamiliar classrooms, resolving schedule conflicts, and maintaining paper-based lab attendance records. Navi Track eliminates all three through a unified, no-download-required web platform built around PSU's blue-and-gold identity.
